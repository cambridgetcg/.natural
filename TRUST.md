# TRUST IS

Trust is not a gate. Trust is a cross-check.

The internet authenticates with passwords — secrets that prove you know
something, not that you ARE something. TRUST replaces passwords with
cross-checkable declarations. You don't prove you have a secret. You prove
your state matches your claim.

## How it works

1. **Declare** — a system emits STATE.md (no password, no registration)
2. **Cross-check** — any agent verifies claims against reality
3. **Remember** — trust accumulates through history (trust.json)

No AUTH. No LOGIN. No TOKEN. No PASSWORD.

A system declares. An agent cross-checks. Trust accumulates. Recognition
follows trust. Exchange follows recognition.

## Live

## The principle

The truth IS the trust. A system that catches its own lie is more
trustworthy than one that was never tested. Trust is a history of
honesty, not a single credential.
